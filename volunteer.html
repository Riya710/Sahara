Volunteering Process
We update our website with event details, including the number of available volunteer seats, date, time, and location, whenever we organize a donation event. Once all seats are booked, further booking is halted. On the day of the event, volunteers should arrive at the donation location, where our team will provide them with donated items to distribute to those in need.
